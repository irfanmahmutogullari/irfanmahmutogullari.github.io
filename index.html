About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Declarative Languages and Artificial Intelligence (DTAI) group at the Department of Computer Science at KU Leuven. I am working on better integration of machine learning techniques and stochastic optimization methods in the context of the ERC-funded project Conversational human-aware technology for optimization (CHAT-Opt) in Prof. Tias Guns’ lab.
