I am an assistant professor at Department of Industrial Engineering, TED University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Industrial Engineering, Bilkent University in 2019. During my Ph.D. study, I was a visiting scholar at H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology from October 2017 to July 2018. My research focuses on developing efficient solution methods for stochastic programming and robust optimization models. I am also interested in application of these models to the problems emerging from different areas of operations research.
